About Twintify

Twintify is a specialised practice founded by Ramesh Balasubramanian, an architect and geomatics engineer. We combine architectural insight with digital surveying and modelling to provide reliable digital twins for projects across conservation, architecture, and immersion.

Divisions & Services

Our work is organised into four divisions so clients can clearly find what they need.

Twintify: Art

Digital Asset Creation (3D modeling)

High-fidelity 3D models for design, visualization and game-ready assets.

Digitising Physical Assets (Photogrammetry)

Accurate capture of artworks, sculptures and objects for preservation and reuse.

Digital Asset Curation

Organising and optimising assets for integration into projects or pipelines.

Twintify: Architecture

Architecture & Interior Design

Design that responds to context, craft and longevity.

Adaptive Reuse & Surveying

Our goals

Twintify focuses on measurable outcomes that support conservation, long-term value and immersion.

1. Heritage Conservation

Preserve, restore, extend life, or enable adaptive reuse of valuable historic buildings through 3D cultural heritage informatics—equipping conservators with the data needed for informed decisions.

2. Future-Sustaining Design

Design, create and recreate durable, value-rich buildings rooted in traditional and scientific principles—buildings intended to hold cultural and material value for centuries.

3. Immersive Environments

Support storytellers and virtual world developers in building photorealistic environments—an essential component of meaningful immersion.
