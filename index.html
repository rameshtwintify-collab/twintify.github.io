About Us

Twintify is a specialised practice founded by Ramesh Balasubramanian, an architect and geomatics engineer. We combine architectural insight with digital surveying and modelling to provide reliable digital twins for projects across conservation, architecture, and immersion.

Our Goals

1. Heritage Conservation

Preserve, restore, extend life, or enable adaptive reuse of valuable historic buildings through 3D cultural heritage informatics.

2. Future-Sustaining Design

Design and create durable, value-rich buildings rooted in traditional and scientific principles—structures that endure for centuries.

3. Immersive Environments

Support storytellers and XR developers in building photorealistic environments for meaningful immersion.
